Highlight the text of an article with your cursor, it will cause annotation widget to appear. In the annotation widget you need to specify the tag of the component, which, in this task, is only MajorClaim. The widget has auto-completion, thus, when you start typing, a tag suggestion will appear and you can choose it. Finally, click OK button.

In order to delete the highlighted component first select the already highlighted text and in the widget click icon.

When you are satisfied with your annotations click Submit button in the bottom left corner to finish the main annotation task.
