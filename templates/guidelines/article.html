Annotation guidelines

In this task you will need to highlight major claim occurrences in the text.

Be sure to dedicate a good portion of the given time to carefully read all the sections below before you proceed with the annotation.

Leave a few minutes for a short feedback questionnaire after the main annotation task.

Components:

  • MajorClaim: highlighting example.

  • ClaimFor: highlighting example.

  • ClaimAgainst: highlighting example.

  • Premise: highlighting example.

Relations: Support, Attack.

The last 3 types of components as well as both types of relations are not part of this task, therefore, should not be annotated. Only MajorClaim occurrences should be annotated.
