Introduction

In this task you will be provided with a section from an article on the topics of sustainable diets, veganism, meat alternatives, effects of food production on the environment, and others.

Your task is to identify argument components present in the text and determine how they are related to each other as well as the main view of the article's author.

Likely, you will have your own opinions and may agree or disagree with the contents of the articles, finding some statements to be untrue or even offensive. However, in order to correctly complete the task, it is crucial that you stay objective and follow the described guidelines to the best of your ability.

Motivation

Your work may eventually be used in developing Artificial Intelligence (AI) that is capable of automatically extracting arguments and their relations from the text.

Extracted arguments can then be used for various applications. For instance, identifying main arguments that people use for switching/not switching to sustainable diets. Such information could then be used for improving communication between governments and citizens to encourage more sustainable dietary choices. Alternatively, food industry may leverage this information in order to develop new and attractive plant-based products or improve qualities of the existing ones.
